About Us

INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND

Energy and Power Engineering Solutions (EPES) PTY LTD is a 100% black owned leading provider of cutting-edge energy and power solutions

With a strong commitment to innovation and sustainability, EPES is dedicated to revolutionizing the energy and power industry by providing custom made solutions that meet specific client needs.

EPES provides professional services in planning,designing,construction,commissioning and maintenance of power generation, transmission and distribution of systems . Our solutions come from experts with a combined industrial experience of more than 60 years.

EPES was founded in 2022 by Engineer Martin Kanyepe ,a Professional Engineer registered with Engineering Council of South Africa (ECSA) and Engineering Council of Zimbabwe (ECZ). Martin holds an Msc in Renewable Energy,B.Eng (Hons) in Renewable Energy,B.Tech (Hons) in Electrical Engineering and a Diploma in General Management.
